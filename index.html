About Tuynuk

Tuynuk is an open-source file transfer application designed to make sharing files seamless and secure. Built using Flutter, Tuynuk ensures that your files are transferred swiftly while maintaining end-to-end encryption to safeguard your data. Whether you're sending a small document or a large media file, Tuynuk handles it all with ease.

Features

  • End-to-End Encryption: All files are encrypted before transfer, ensuring that only the intended recipient can access them.
  • Fast Transfer Speeds: Optimized for quick transfers, even for large files.
  • Cross-Platform: Available on multiple platforms, allowing you to share files across different devices.
  • Open Source: Tuynuk is open source, meaning you can view, modify, and contribute to the code.
